Island Hotels/Lodging

Hostel

Affordable, social, and steps from the beach—Driftwood Island Hostel is the perfect home base for travelers chasing sun and adventure. With cozy dorms, private rooms, and a breezy common area, it's where new friendships and unforgettable island memories are made.

Phone Number: 1-800-HOS-TEL1

Hotels

Ocean Pearl Resort

Indulge in island luxury at Ocean Pearl Resort, where oceanfront suites, infinity pools, and spa treatments redefine relaxation. Perfect for couples and honeymooners seeking romance and tranquility in paradise.

Phone Number: 1-800-PEA-RLRE

Sunset Bay Hotel

Charming and affordable, Sunset Bay Hotel offers cozy rooms with stunning sunset views. Located near local shops and beaches, it’s a favorite for travelers who want comfort and convenience without the big resort price tag.

Phone Number: 1-800-SUN-SET1

Palm Crest All-Inclusive

Everything you need, all in one place. Palm Crest offers all-inclusive dining, activities, and entertainment for families and groups. From kids’ clubs to nightly shows, it’s the ultimate fun-in-the-sun destination.

Phone Number: 1-800-PAL-MCRE

The Hideaway Villas

Tucked in beautiful water and steps from a private beach, The Hideaway Villas provide a secluded, boutique escape. With just a few high-end bungalows, it’s ideal for those seeking privacy, nature, and a slower pace.

Phone Number: 1-800-VIL-LAS1
