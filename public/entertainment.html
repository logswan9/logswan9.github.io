Fun is just the beginning on Taniti

Ziplining

Soar through the treetops and experience the thrill of ziplining on a tropical island adventure! With breathtaking views, gentle breezes, and exciting zips through lush canopies, it's a fun and safe activity for the whole family.

Phone Number: 1-800-ZIP-AWAY

Snorkeling

Dive into a world of wonder with snorkeling on a tropical island! Explore vibrant coral reefs, swim alongside colorful fish, and discover the magic beneath the waves. Fun for the whole family.

Phone Number: 1-800-DIV-4FUN

Golfing

Tee off in paradise at Coral Cove Golf Club, where lush fairways meet ocean breezes. This scenic 18-hole course offers stunning coastal views, challenging play, and a relaxing clubhouse to unwind after your round. Perfect for golfers of all skill levels looking for a tropical twist on the game.

Phone Number: 1-800-TEE-OFF1

Volcano Tour

Get up close to the island’s fiery heart with Lava Ridge Volcano Tours. Hike ancient lava trails, learn the island’s explosive history, and take in breathtaking views from the summit. It's an unforgettable journey through one of nature’s most powerful wonders.

Phone Number: 1-800-ASH-COVE
