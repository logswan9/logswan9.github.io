Restaurants

Dockside Diner

Craving comfort food with a view? Dockside Diner serves up all-American classics like juicy burgers, crispy fries, and homemade apple pie—right by the water’s edge. It’s casual, friendly, and perfect for the whole family after a day in the sun.

Phone Number: 1-800-FOO-DNOW

The Island Grillhouse

BBQ and beach vibes collide at The Island Grillhouse. Dig into smoky ribs, grilled corn, and loaded mac & cheese while sipping cold lemonade under swaying palms. Laid-back, delicious, and unmistakably American.

Phone Number: 1-801-FOO-DNOW

Mango Breeze Café

Get a taste of the tropics at Mango Breeze Café. From coconut shrimp and mango salsa to fresh passionfruit smoothies, every dish brings out the island’s vibrant flavors in a chill, open-air setting.

Phone Number: 1-802-FOO-DNOW

Tiki Tide Kitchen

Immerse yourself in true island dining at Tiki Tide Kitchen, where pineapple-glazed chicken, plantain chips, and rum-infused sauces meet ocean views and tiki torch-lit evenings. A tropical escape for your taste buds.

Phone Number: 1-803-FOO-DNOW

Island Fresh Market

Your one-stop shop for island living! Island Fresh Market offers local produce, fresh-caught seafood, and everyday essentials—all with a friendly, laid-back feel. Whether you're prepping a beach picnic or stocking your rental, we’ve got you covered.

Phone Number: 1-800-MAR-KET1

Paradise Provisions

From tropical fruits to travel-size sunscreen, Paradise Provisions carries everything you need for life in paradise. Enjoy a mix of local flavors, international favorites, and convenient grab-and-go options for your island adventures.

Phone Number: 1-800-MAR-KET2
